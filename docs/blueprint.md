# **App Name**: Cela Pay Gateway

## Core Features:

- Seller Storefront & Product Management: Sellers can onboard their business, manage their profile, and list products or services with pricing. This involves setting up categories and individual product details.
- Buyer Product Browsing & Quote Request: Buyers can browse available products from sellers and submit detailed quote requests including buyer name, desired quantity, and a message to the seller.
- Smart Quote Notes Tool: Utilize AI to suggest professional and contextually relevant notes for sellers to include in their quotes, aiding communication and efficiency.
- Dynamic Transaction Status Tracking: Provides real-time updates and clear visual indicators for both buyers and sellers on the current status of each transaction (e.g., Pending, Awaiting Quote, Paid, Completed, Declined).
- Seller Quote Generation & Bank Details: Sellers can review buyer requests, specify a quote amount, provide bank transfer details, and send the quote for buyer acceptance.
- Payment Confirmation Flow: A streamlined process for buyers to mark payment as submitted, and for sellers to verify payment receipt and confirm the transaction's completion.
- Transaction Records & Analytics: Sellers have access to a dashboard displaying historical transaction records, including statuses, and basic statistics like total revenue.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: A very deep, desaturated warm grey (#161514). It creates a sophisticated and unobtrusive base, aligning with a tech-forward financial application.
- Primary color: A rich, golden amber (#D8A62A). This is used for interactive elements, highlights, and primary actions, providing a dynamic yet trustworthy feel.
- Accent color: A vibrant, balanced emerald green (#26A666). Used to indicate success, completion, and positive feedback, providing a clear visual contrast for crucial confirmations.
- Headline font: 'Syne', sans-serif (used for prominent titles and branding, leveraging its strong and modern aesthetic). Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Body font: 'DM Sans', sans-serif (used for general text throughout the application for its clear readability and contemporary appeal). Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Utilize modern, clean, and bold outlined icons to represent features and statuses clearly, maintaining a consistent aesthetic with the contemporary design.
- Implement a two-column, responsive layout optimized for clear separation of Buyer and Seller interfaces, using cards and panels to group related information.
- Incorporate subtle hover effects on interactive elements and soft transitions for state changes, enhancing user feedback without being distracting.
